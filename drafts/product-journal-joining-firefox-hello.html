Product Journal: Joining Firefox Hello

Tool: we’ll make a tool. After a while we realized it really wasn’t a tool. In fact the ideas surrounding tools were wrong, because they are things that stir existing media. What you really want is to, if what you have is something that is basically a new medium, is like paper now, then you can create tools that stir that particular medium.

– Alan Kay: Computer Applications: A Dynamic Medium for Creative Thought 1972

Maybe my sense of a medium: how can we empower people to communication things they could not communicate before? How do we make this a great way to say something? I believe the power of Hello is in how it serves as a conduit of expression, and how it helps organize thought. I.e., a medium.
