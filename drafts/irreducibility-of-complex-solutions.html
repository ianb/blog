The Irreducibility of Complex Solutions

I was listening to an AI researcher talk about how most material was published only to Arxiv, and how it was useful for discussing early results, small incremental results, more like a discussion than the pace of normal academic journals. He also noted that negative results didn’t mean anything for AI. Even being unable to reproduce a result was fairly unimportant, because it’s so easy to just get something wrong and ruin a result.
