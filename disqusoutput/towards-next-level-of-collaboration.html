Nice write-up, really appreciate you sharing your perspective on collaboration in such depth.

One quick thing I wanted to comment on, Sqwiggle isn’t necessarily about showing emotion to your computer, it’s about providing ambient awareness with your team, letting you know beyond a doubt whether or not the person is at their computer and available.

Though I would also argue that there is an element of emotion. We have several customers who have told us how great it is to see their team member’s reactions to a gif posted in the stream, or a failed / successful build, etc. It definitely adds more than a simple presence indicator.
